Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Ryan, I am an American who loves to travel. I visit China about 2 times a year because of how beautiful and diverse the country is.

Why do I have this blog?

Anyone who has tried to plan for a trip to China has probably noticed the lack of resources on the internet. Many people still view it as a mysterious place that seems untouchable, but I want to change that.

I have experienced the troubles on getting a visa, transportation, payments, and much more. I want to share my experience and have a place where real current knowledge is shared.
