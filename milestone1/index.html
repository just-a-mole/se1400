Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

Articles focusing on tips and tricks for travling in China. Including how to get the visa, how to book things, apps to get, etc.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will write the content myself, and use pictures of my travels in China or screenshots from my phone for the apps.

Why should users care about the focus on the site?

It can be intimidating to travel to China, because there are lots of things to be prepared for such as a visa or payment methods. Having a website that helps prepare you is very helpful for potential tourists.

Why should a site be built for this?

A website is the most efficient way to spread travling information, as it can get lost in social media.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I want them to come here when the google "how to get a visa for china" or other topics like that. I want them to come here and get information, and potentially click on affilate links for hotels or VPNs.

What is the goal of the site

My goal is to educate and give high quality and current information for people to have a good and easy time to get to China.
